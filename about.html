I want people to feel comfortable and included regardless of skill level.

At its core, gaming should be about fun, adventure, and just escaping the norms of everyday life. As a casual player, I understand how intimidating it can be to approach other players. We've all probably experienced a rage quitter or toxic gamers at some point. The competitive "ultra sweaty" crowd is certainly entitled to their own outlets, but can be a bit unforgiving to those of us who just want to focus on fun.

Does this mean we don't care about objectives or leaderboards? Not at all! We just prefer to game with like-minded individuals with a more "no sweat" relaxed approach to things. We prove that you can still have organized squads, learn as you go, and have fun even when things go sideways. So let's get each other added to our respective friends lists and get to it!
